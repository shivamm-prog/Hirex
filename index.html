Payments, refunds & cancellation
Payment flow (applies everywhere)
  • Paid listings show Proceed to Payment.
  • Checkout is simulated (UI only).
  • After success you’ll see: Payment Successful, Registration Confirmed, and receipt message.
Refund policy
  • Refund allowed only until the event starts.
  • After the event starts → no refund possible.
  • If organizer cancels → full refund issued automatically.
Cancellation terms
  • You can cancel registration before event start.
  • Organizer cancellations trigger automatic refunds.
  • Cancellation status is clearly displayed in your registration state.
